How To Use


In the yellow box, enter the time it takes you to run two different distances. Then, make a selection from the dropdown menu. Estimate your potential in the chosen distance by clicking submit.

With proper use, the calculator can be accurate to within one percent. Read the Best Practices to ensure you get realistic results.
